# 74. Problem Title

## 📋 Problem Overview

Consider MetroDelivery, a regional distribution company operating in a major metropolitan area. Each morning, their fleet of 25 delivery trucks departs from the central distribution center carrying various products to be delivered to 150 retail customers throughout the city. However, the logistics landscape has evolved beyond simple one-way deliveries.

## 🎯 Solution Approaches

### **Tier 1**: The Pen & Paper Method (Network Flow Formulation) — [`P74-Tier-1_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-1_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Complete directed graph with known travel costs between all locations
  - Vehicle capacity is uniform and limited


### **Tier 2**: The Classic Heuristic (Priority-Based Construction) — [`P74-Tier-2_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-2_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Dynamic priority calculation based on multiple factors (distance, demand, urgency)
  - Two-stage construction: deliveries first, then pickups


### **Tier 3**: The Advanced Algorithm (Dragonfly Optimization) — [`P74-Tier-3_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-3_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Population-based swarm intelligence with multiple dragonflies
  - Five behavioral factors: separation, alignment, cohesion, food attraction, enemy distraction


### **Tier 4**: The AI/ML/RL Augmentation Method (Self-Supervised Learning) — [`P74-Tier-4_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-4_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Self-supervised learning without requiring manually labeled training data
  - Graph Transformer architecture for processing customer relationships


### **Tier 5**: The Integrated Digital Twin (System-of-Systems Simulation) — [`P74-Tier-5_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-5_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Real-time synchronization between physical and virtual logistics ecosystems
  - Multi-layered architecture: Vehicle Fleet, Customer Demand, Traffic Network, Warehouse Operations, Driver Performance


### **Tier 6**: The Autonomous & Self-Optimizing Ecosystem (Distributed Intelligence) — [`P74-Tier-6_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-6_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Distributed decision-making among intelligent autonomous agents
  - Three agent types: Vehicle Agents, Customer Agents, Coordinator Agents


### **Tier 7**: Human-AI Symbiotic Partnership — [`P74-Tier-7_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-7_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Human-AI collaborative decision-making with explainable AI recommendations
  - Trust-based relationship building between human experts and AI systems


### **Tier 8**: The Value-Aligned & Ethical Framework — [`P74-Tier-8_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-8_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Multi-stakeholder utility optimization balancing economic, social, and environmental objectives
  - Constitutional AI with embedded ethical principles and value alignment constraints


### **Tier 9**: The Quantum Leap (Computational Supremacy) — [`P74-Tier-9_executed.ipynb`](./P74-Tier-9_executed.ipynb)
- **Learning Goals**:
  - Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA) for combinatorial optimization
  - QUBO formulation enabling quantum annealing and gate-based quantum computing
